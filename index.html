About LCC

Lotus Convention Centre (LCC) is one of the ORIGINAL and LEADING convention centers in Bangladesh. It was established in 2003 with the goal of providing the highest standard of services in the Hospitality Industry. Lotus Convention Centre offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central business district of Gulshan, Dhaka.
